Airline Pilot Turned Software Engineer

About Me

I am from Narragansett, RI and have always had a passion for creating apps, games, and software. I began making Android apps in 2015 and have thousands of downloads in the Google Play Store. In 2018 I became a flight instructor and taught people how to fly small airplanes. I later became an Airline Pilot flying United Airlines E-145 jets. After COVID-19, I got deep into web development and returned to school. I earned a Bachelors in Computer Science from the University of Rhode Island. My goal is to become part of a team and contribute to the development of something amazing.

Bachelor's in Computer Science

In December 2020, I earned a Bachelor's in Computer Science from the University of Rhode Island. My studies have included: Algorithms and Data Structures, Database Management, Object Orientated Programming, Operating Systems and Networks, Secure Programming, and Software Engineering.
